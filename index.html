Our Business

Maison International is a leading and rapid growing groceries operator and integrator with a focus on the minority market in the United States. We operate an online-and-offline integrated on-demand retail business offering fresh produce, meat, seafood, and other daily necessities through a convenient and excellent shopping experience supported by our innovative, technology empowered omni-channel business model. We are committed to make grocery shopping enjoyable, affordable, efficient and trustworthy.

Our mission statement is to make buying fresh groceries as easy as breathing fresh air. We operate supermarkets in multiple locations throughout LA, California, including Alhambra, San Gabriel, El Monte, Monrovia, and Monterey Park. Shown below are snapshots of our stores in operation.

Store Network

We operate two types of stores – the center store and the satellite store.

The center stores, with full digital capabilities, mainly serve the traditional family-oriented customers to provide state-to-the-art store design and a variety of fresh produce and daily necessities at competitive prices.

The satellite stores are to penetrate local communities and neighbors with heavy population of younger customers (Millennials, Gen Z), such as universities and colleges. The satellite stores provide selective SKUs that are popular and trendy on social media to attract and adopt the concept of “Meal Solution Super Market” or MSSM that suits the lifestyle of Millennials and Gen Z’s. The satellite stores are defined for customers to shop for experience and lifestyle.

Supply Chain System

Our omni-channel business model reaches beyond grocery operations. We integrate both upstream and downstream to create a one-stop-shop customer shopping experience that brings fresh grocery shopping as easy as breathing fresh air. We run our own import / export business and also partner with reputable suppliers to ensure consistent and high-quality supplies and continued SKU refreshes to bring in top, trendy products. Our center-satellite store network gives us the ability to set up in-store, mini-warehouses to achieve fast order fulfillment and speedy delivery. Combined with our apps and store network, the customers can shop traditionally in store, order online for delivery or in-store pick up, however they like.
