About Us

As the market evolves, new innovators are emerging in the Asian supermarket industry, traditional supermarkets such as Good Fortune and Hong Kong supermarkets face unprecedented challenges. The current shopping experience struggles to keep customers, and the lack of online arm directly leads to the loss of young consumer groups.

To save the long-lasting supermarkets, Maison carried out a series of evaluation and analyses of both supermarkets and officially launched the merger and transformation initiative.

Maison acquired a total of 4 stores of Good Fortune and Hong Kong Supermarkets in California in 2020 and renamed them HK Good Fortune Supermarkets, to kick-off a series of changes.

Followed the completion of the acquisition, Maison initiated the Omni-channel transformation to upgrade the traditional grocery business.
